Pain Bubbles Content
Each bubble is a real Indian family pain: WhatsApp chaos, bill disputes, health reminders, scattered photos. These are clickable/animated in the real page — they pulse and float gently, then a new one replaces an old one every 3 seconds.
Animation Intent
Bubbles float with subtle CSS keyframe animation (up-down drift, slight rotation). The family silhouette glows orange warmly in the center. On scroll, bubbles "fade away" and the headline transitions to the solution — chaos → order.
CTA Strategy
Email capture (not "Get Started" to auth) — pre-launch waitlist. Simple, low friction. Social proof strip reinforces with avatar count + trust signals.
Mobile Adaptation
On mobile, bubbles stack vertically in 2 columns above the headline. Family silhouette becomes smaller centered icon. CTA stacks vertically with full-width button.
